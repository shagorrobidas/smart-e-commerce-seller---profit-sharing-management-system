The Gap in E-Commerce.

Marketplace dashboards like Daraz or CartUp focus on sales execution, not business governance. Most sellers rely on fragmented spreadsheets, leading to financial inaccuracies and disputes.

  • No centralized financial database.
  • Manual tracking of expenses and platform fees.
  • Opacity in investor profit sharing.

The Solution

A lightweight ERP tailored for sellers. We bridge the gap by automating the "Business" side of e-commerce—from accurate P&L calculation to transparent investor reporting.
